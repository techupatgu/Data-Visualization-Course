## Course in scientific visualization (03-06 Nov 2025)

### Assignment
The assignment for those who take the visualization course for credit consists in:

1) Produce a visualization based on data from your dissertation project or other data
2) Explain the thinking behind it – what you want to achieve and how. This should include a short description of the dataset.
3) Citing the assigned readings, critically examine the visualization you have produced – is it as accurate and helpful as possible?
4) Hand in **no later than 21 November**
